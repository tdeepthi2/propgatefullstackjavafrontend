About Appreciation Entity

The Appreciation entity tracks changes in property valuation over time. Real estate assets grow or fluctuate in market value based on local demand, development, inflation, and renovation. Recording historical valuation shifts ensures we can compute accurate annual yield indicators, capital gains metrics, and net portfolio equity values.

Appreciation Details:

Three core elements comprise appreciation records. The Previous Value tracks property valuation before the recalculation event. The Current Value logs the newly appraised property valuation. The Appreciation Rate (%) represents the rate of value growth relative to the previous valuation.

Valuation Adjustment Triggers & Mechanics

Asset appreciation tracking ensures the platform reflects correct portfolio equity values over time. The Growth Metric calculation is computed as ((currentValue - previousValue) / previousValue) * 100 to verify the exact rate percentage change. The Appraisal Triggers update asset valuations due to professional site appraisals, physical improvements and capital expenditures, or general market rate adjustments. Finally, through Real-time Propagation, modifying property valuation triggers automatic recalculations of total portfolio asset values, historical charts, and net capital gain projections.
