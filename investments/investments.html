About Investments Entity

The Investment entity tracks the allocation of capital from investors into specific properties. Each investment links an investor (User) to a target Property, establishing the capital basis used to compute portfolio performance, individual asset ROI, and equity ownership shares.

Investment Lifecycle:

An investment shifts through three functional status levels. When ACTIVE, the capital has been deployed and the investor is actively earning returns, rental dividends, and capital appreciation. When PENDING, the transaction is undergoing review, Escrow verification, or contract signing. Once COMPLETED, the investment cycle has concluded, the asset has been liquidated or bought out, and capital gains have been distributed.

Capital Allocation & Yield Distribution

Investments model the fractional or sole ownership of physical property assets. Through Fractional Co-Ownership, PropGate supports linking multiple investors to a single property, allowing fractional equity shares based on relative capital contributions. For Dividend Distribution, rental payouts are calculated and routed to corresponding investor ledger accounts pro-rata to their active investment holdings. Finally, the Capital Lock-in mechanism triggers when status transitions from PENDING to ACTIVE, locking in capital and updating the investor's total portfolio net asset value calculation.
