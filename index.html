About PropGate

PropGate is a comprehensive real estate management system developed to streamline the management of properties, users, and real estate operations through a role-based platform. It provides separate functionalities for investors, agents, and administrators, ensuring that each user can efficiently perform their responsibilities. Investors can manage their property portfolios, record expenses, and monitor financial performance through analytics such as Return on Investment (ROI), Rental Yield, and Property Health Score, enabling informed investment decisions. Agents can create and manage property listings, making it easier to market available properties.
